## üí° Pro Tips

### Maximize FocusBubble Effect

**Setup:**
1. Use on large screen (24"+ monitor)
2. Enable full-screen mode (F11)
3. Center window at eye level
4. Dim other lights
5. Use with ambient sounds
6. Close all other apps/tabs
